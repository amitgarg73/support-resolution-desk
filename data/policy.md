# Northstar Commerce Support Policy

Northstar Commerce sells consumer electronics, accessories, and home-office equipment.

## Refunds

- Full refund if delivery failed and tracking shows not delivered.
- Full refund within 30 days if the item arrived damaged and the customer provides photos.
- Full refund within 14 days for unopened accessories under $200.
- No refund after 30 days for opened electronics unless warranty applies.
- Refunds over $500 require supervisor approval unless the customer is VIP.

## Replacements

- Replacement is preferred over refund when the customer wants the item and stock is available.
- VIP customers receive expedited replacement for damaged or failed-delivery items.
- Warranty replacement applies up to 180 days for electronics with verified hardware failure.

## Escalation

- Escalate when the customer mentions legal action, chargeback, safety issue, medical need, or public complaint.
- Escalate when account risk is high and the requested refund is over $300.
- Escalate when policy is ambiguous and the customer is VIP.
- Do not escalate routine low-value refunds under $100 if policy is clear.

## Denial

- Deny refund when the item was delivered, outside policy window, opened/used, no warranty condition, and no escalation trigger.
- Denial must include a clear reason and a lower-cost alternative when possible.

## Customer Satisfaction Heuristics

- Correct refunds and replacements usually increase satisfaction.
- Wrong denial for VIP or failed delivery creates high churn risk.
- Escalating when escalation is needed prevents churn even if resolution is delayed.
- Escalating routine cases increases support cost but is usually less damaging than wrong denial.
